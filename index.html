HOW TO PLAY

Just like in real music, there’s a key signature card drawn at the start of the game that everyone obeys to know what note cards to play and not to play . Every turn, you may place down up to three note cards, in the hopes of combining them to form chords! (Diatonic triads)

The first to collect 3 chords is the winner, the bringer of harmony, the Lord of the Chords!

But wait, there’s more! Instead of playing note cards, you can play hilarious action cards full of musical puns to skip your friends’ turns by placing them “Under A Rest! ” with Rest Cards, deny your friends the win with “CHORD BLOCK” cards, or forcefully steal your friends’ action cards with cards like “Gimme Debussy” and "Major 7ths"!
