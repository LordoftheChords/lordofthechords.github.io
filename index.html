What is ETHSingapore?

+

ETHSingapore is the ASEAN’s largest Ethereum hackathon, where developers of all backgrounds will work on projects using Ethereum that can have an impact on the world. We enable teams to make something great in only 30 hours by providing an abundance of hacking resources like mentors, sponsors, and software. Sleeping is optional, but trying to make awesome things is not.
